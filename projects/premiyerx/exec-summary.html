The business problem

Good LinkedIn content is hard to do consistently. It takes time to think of a topic, write a strong hook, structure the post for mobile readers, add supporting data, create a graphic, and publish at the right time. Most people do not have time to do all of that every day.

The solution

This app turns that whole process into a guided flow. It gives you a clean interface, recommends what to post, generates high-quality text and visuals, and packages everything so you can publish faster with more confidence.

What we built

One-click post generationLive

Pick a topic, choose a format, and generate a polished post in seconds with a hook, body, CTA, hashtags, and a first comment.

AI-powered writingLive

Optional OpenAI support generates original posts in Prem’s voice using current news, market context, and proven creator-style structures.

Algorithm scoringLive

Each post is scored for hook quality, readability, structure, credibility, CTA quality, and other signals tied to LinkedIn performance.

Carousel PDF generatorLive

The app converts posts into clean LinkedIn document carousels with a cover, content slides, one dedicated sources page, and a branded closing slide.

Fact-check gateNew

Before posting, the app now scans every number and claim, flags anything unverified or stale, and requires confirmation before it moves forward.

Data registryNew

All important stats are stored in one place with source information and verification dates so outdated numbers can be caught and corrected quickly.

Design and usability improvements

We also redesigned the experience to feel more premium and intuitive. The flow is faster, the buttons are clearer, the dark visual design is more polished, and the app works as a desktop and mobile installable PWA.

Why this matters

The biggest shift was moving from “generate something” to “generate something that can actually perform.” We researched what the best LinkedIn creators do, then built those patterns into the engine.

We followed creators, not celebrities

We studied major LinkedIn names and found an important difference: some people get reach because they are famous, while others earn it through writing skill and consistency. Since Prem is building reach through content quality, we modeled the app after successful creators like Justin Welsh, Jasmin Alic, and Lara Acosta instead of celebrity CEOs.

What changed in the writing engine

  • Short, stronger opening hooks designed to stop scrolling.
  • Cleaner formatting with line breaks, bullets, arrows, and light emoji use.
  • Clearer calls to action that invite comments instead of sounding promotional.
  • First-comment generation to help boost engagement after posting.
  • Better use of carousels and visuals because those formats often outperform plain text.

Bottom line

This is no longer just an MVP. It is now a practical, polished content system designed to save time, improve quality, reduce errors, and increase the odds that each post performs well on LinkedIn.

In plain English: the app now helps Prem create smarter posts, faster, with better design, stronger writing, and much better protection against sharing inaccurate information publicly.
